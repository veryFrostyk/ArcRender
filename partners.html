Partnership Terms

How ArcRender works with brands.

Straightforward terms established upfront. No surprises, no scope creep, no manufactured enthusiasm for products that are not a fit.

What works
  • + Paid integrations within existing long-form videos
  • + Products that genuinely fit the channel's tech and gadget focus
  • + Review units for larger electronics with full editorial independence
  • + Direct brand contact only, no agencies
  • + Segment review for factual accuracy before publish
  • + Permission to boost published content for 3 months
What does not work
  • x Gifting-only collaborations with no compensation
  • x Dedicated sponsored videos or dedicated Shorts
  • x Talent agency or management inquiries
  • x Script approval or editorial control over content
  • x Products outside the channel's core interest areas
  • x Guaranteed positive reviews
Review Units for Larger Products

For significant electronics launches, including laptops, cameras, phones, handhelds, and other hardware, ArcRender is open to accepting review units under the condition that all commentary, opinions, and conclusions remain fully independent. No exceptions.

All thoughts expressed in ArcRender videos are the creator's own. A review unit does not guarantee coverage, a positive review, or any specific framing of the product.
